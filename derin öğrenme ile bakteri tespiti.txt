Then we researched the technologies used for this, the competence required, the time it took to build and develop and the cost. Endless thanks to Dr. Nesrin AYDIN ATASOY, for her valuable information and support to me and my projectmate on this subject. 

Our project was about deep learning. There are two important issues in this project. The first is to create an artificial neural network model. The second is dataset preparation. I developed an artificial neural network model. My projectmate researched dataset preparation and noise removal techniques. I reviewed the codes shared on the forums and attended online courses. The created neural network can trained on the computer's CPU, but it takes too long. Because CPUs are designed to run in saving mode. So I trained the model on the GPU. I used Tensorflow library and Nvidia graphics card with Tensorflow support for training. I installed cuda and cudnn what compatible with my GPU. I used the Python programming language and the Spyder IDE. 

I trained my models that I changed and tried many times. It took me two months to prepare the most suitable model for my project. I finally made it a desktop program. I used the PyQt5 library for this.
